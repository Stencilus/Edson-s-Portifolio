Key Research

Effects of Exchange Rate and Interest Rates Changes on Loan Repayments

A Quantitative Study on Zimbabwe (2019 - 2021)

One of the most significant revenue-generating sectors in banking is loan offices. The loan book provides 75 percent of the bank's revenue in a normal financial structure. This study primarily looked at two macroeconomic elements to see how changes in exchange rates and interest rates affect loan repayments.

Conclusion: Exchange rates, high interest rates, GDP levels, and unemployment have a significant impact on repayments. The researcher suggests interest rate adjustments should align with consumer income status to avoid default during currency depreciation.
